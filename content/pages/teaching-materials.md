## Lecture Notes & Slides

### Data Science for Business

**Introduction to Machine Learning** (PDF, 45 slides)  
Conceptual introduction to supervised learning, model evaluation, and the bias-variance tradeoff. Designed for students with no ML background.

**Neural Networks Without Calculus** (PDF, 38 slides)  
Visual explanation of how neural networks work, using interactive diagrams and business examples.

**Ethics in Data Science** (PDF, 52 slides)  
Case studies on algorithmic bias, privacy, and fairness. Includes discussion questions and exercises.

### Advanced Financial Accounting with AI

**NLP for Financial Disclosure** (PDF, 41 slides)  
Introduction to natural language processing with accounting applications. Covers sentiment analysis, topic modeling, and entity recognition.

**Explainable AI for Audit** (PDF, 36 slides)  
Why interpretability matters in high-stakes decisions. Methods for making black-box models transparent.

## Code Repositories

**[Financial Statement Analysis Tutorials](https://github.com/example/fsa-python)**  
Jupyter notebooks teaching financial analysis with Python. Includes data acquisition from EDGAR, ratio analysis, and visualization.{% sidenote "sn-fsa" "Used by 50+ instructors at other universities." %}

**[ML for Accounting Problems](https://github.com/example/ml-accounting)**  
Complete code for common ML applications in accounting: fraud detection, going concern prediction, audit risk assessment.

**[Blockchain Examples](https://github.com/example/blockchain-edu)**  
Simple blockchain implementations in Python for educational purposes. Starts from scratch with proof-of-work, then adds features.

## Suggested Readings

**For Instructors New to Data Science**

- *Introduction to Statistical Learning* (James et al.) — Accessible, math-light introduction
- *Python for Data Analysis* (McKinney) — Practical guide to pandas and data manipulation
- *Fairness and Machine Learning* (Barocas, Hardt, Narayanan) — Essential reading on AI ethics

**For Instructors Teaching AI in Accounting**

- *Artificial Intelligence in Accounting* (Kokina & Davenport)
- *Continuous Auditing* (Alles et al.)
- Recent papers in Journal of Emerging Technologies in Accounting
