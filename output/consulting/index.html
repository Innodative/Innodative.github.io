Consulting

Through Innodative, I help organizations navigate the intersection of emerging technologies and business strategy, with particular focus on artificial intelligence, blockchain, and digital transformation.

Advisory Philosophy

Effective technology consulting begins with business problems, not technology solutions. The question isn't whether to adopt AI or blockchainâ€”it's what specific problems these tools might solve, and whether the solutions justify the costs and risks.Technology adoption decisions should be driven by strategic objectives, not fear of missing out.

This problem-first approach avoids technology for its own sake and produces more durable recommendations anchored in business value rather than technical trends.

Areas of Focus

AI Strategy and Governance
Developing coherent approaches to artificial intelligence, including use case identification, risk assessment, vendor evaluation, and policy development.

Blockchain Feasibility Assessment
Evaluating whether distributed ledger technology genuinely fits a business need. Many proposed blockchain applications would work better with traditional databases.Studies suggest fewer than 10% of announced enterprise blockchain projects have moved to production deployment.

Engagement Approach

Typical engagements follow a structured process: Discovery surfaces the organization's current state and constraints through interviews and stakeholder workshops. Analysis translates findings into specific, prioritized recommendations. Roadmap Development sequences recommendations into an actionable plan with resources, timelines, and metrics. Implementation Support provides ongoing guidance during execution.
